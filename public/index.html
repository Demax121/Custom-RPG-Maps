Thanks for coming here. This project has been created for people who would like to host their map website

I`m a TTRPG player and have always wanted to make maps for my players and myself more accessible when playing online, that`s why I`ve been going through a lot of things that could make it possible, and as there are a lot of options none of them have satisfied me enough.

That`s why I have created this whole Custom RPG Maps project using leafletJS and leaflet Sidebarv2 based on the great map of Eberron by John Cardian.
As you can see design is also very similar not to say it`s a ripoff because I like it.



I hope it will come in handy for you dear user. You can modify it however you want or use it as it is I hope you will like what I have done here.
